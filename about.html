About Ernest Fowler

Born in the city of Dallas, Texas, I’ve always had a fire to create. Growing up, I have always loved music, stories, and was a child full of imagination. I spent my free time bringing my random ideas to fruition, such as making board games, websites, video games, and “interactive” paper devices.


In my later years, I grew a fascination for psychology, with a personal interest in the explanation for human motives and behaviors, leading me to study in LSU’s psychology program.


As a UX Designer, I value novel ideas and alternative perspectives, and I want to bring you a product that fulfills your users’ needs, stands out among the crowd, and paves the way for a more accessible tomorrow.
